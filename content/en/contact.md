# Contact

If you have a question, suggestion, comment or recipe idea, you can contact us by email.

Koo-ham.com is a small family project, so we may not always reply immediately, but we will read every message.

You can write to us if you would like to suggest a recipe, report an error, send an idea for improving the website or propose a collaboration related to food, cooking and everyday kitchen life.
