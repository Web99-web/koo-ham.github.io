# Advertise

Koo-ham.com is a small family project with recipes for people who enjoy good, healthy and varied food, but do not always have much time for cooking and food preparation.

The website is made for people looking for practical recipes for real life: quick meals, simple cakes, traditional dishes, healthier ideas and recipes inspired by different cuisines.

## Collaboration options

We are open to collaborations that naturally fit the topics of food, cooking, home life and everyday living.

Possible collaboration options include:

- banner advertising
- sponsored posts
- affiliate partnerships
- product or service features
- collaborations related to food, kitchen appliances, cookware, ingredients, healthy products and lifestyle content

We are especially interested in products and services that make sense for an audience looking for simple, practical and tasty cooking ideas.

## Affiliate and sponsored content

Koo-ham.com may use affiliate links. If a visitor clicks such a link and makes a purchase, we may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to the visitor.

Sponsored content or commercial collaborations will be clearly marked where necessary.
