## Ingredients

- 1 banana  
- 150 g strawberries  
- 150 ml milk  
- 100 g yogurt  
- 1 tablespoon honey  
- a few ice cubes  

## Preparation

1. Wash the strawberries and peel the banana.  
2. Add all ingredients into a blender.  
3. Blend until smooth and creamy.  
4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.  

## Tip

For a colder smoothie, use frozen strawberries or add extra ice cubes before blending.
