# Professional Pancakes

Pancakes just like the ones from a professional pancake bar.

## Ingredients

- 2 eggs  
- 1 teaspoon sugar  
- a pinch of salt  
- a little cinnamon if desired  
- all-purpose flour – by eye  
- milk – by eye  
- a little sparkling water  
- a little oil  

## Preparation

1. First mix together:
   - 2 eggs  
   - 1 teaspoon sugar  
   - a pinch of salt  
   - a little cinnamon if desired  

2. Then add:
   - all-purpose flour  
   - milk  
   - a little sparkling water  
   - a little oil  

3. Mix everything well until the batter is neither too thick nor too runny.  

4. Cook the pancakes on a lightly oiled pan until golden on both sides.  

## Tip

Do not use too much oil in the pan, just enough to lightly coat the surface.
