## Ingredients

- 200 g dark chocolate  
- 120 g butter  
- 150 g sugar  
- 2 eggs  
- 100 g flour  

## Preparation

1. Melt the chocolate and butter together.  
2. Add the sugar and mix well.  
3. Stir in the eggs one at a time.  
4. Add the flour and mix until smooth.  
5. Pour into a baking tray and bake at 180°C for about 25 minutes.  

## Tip

For extra flavor, add chopped walnuts or chocolate chunks before baking.
