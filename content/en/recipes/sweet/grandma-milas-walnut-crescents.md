# Grandma Mila’s Walnut Crescents – the Best Ever

Once you try these walnut crescents, you will keep making them forever.

## Ingredients

- 350 g all-purpose flour  
- 200 g butter  
- margarine can also be used, but the flavor is much worse  
- 80 g powdered sugar  
- 100 g ground walnuts  
- 1 egg white  

## Preparation

1. Mix all ingredients together until you get a smooth dough.  

2. Shape small crescent cookies.  

3. Bake until they become lightly golden.  

4. After baking, roll the crescents in powdered sugar mixed with 2 packets of vanilla sugar.  

## Tip

They taste even better after resting for a few hours because they become softer and more aromatic.

Enjoy your meal!
