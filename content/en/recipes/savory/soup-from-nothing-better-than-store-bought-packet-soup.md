# Soup from ‘Nothing’ – Better Than Store-Bought Packet Soup

Soup made from ‘nothing’ is actually a soup made with Vegeta or a similar seasoning, noodles and eggs.

When you think you have ‘nothing’ for soup, this turns into a surprisingly delicious homemade soup.

## Ingredients

- 2 large tablespoons Vegeta or similar seasoning  
- a handful of noodles  
- 1-2 eggs  
- 2 l water  

## Preparation

1. Heat 2 liters of water and immediately add 2 large tablespoons of Vegeta or similar seasoning.  

2. Once the water starts boiling add a handful of noodles.  

3. After boiling for about 5 minutes, beat 1 or 2 eggs in a separate bowl.  

4. Slowly pour the eggs into the boiling soup while gently stirring.  

5. Let the soup cook for another 5 minutes.  

6. You will see that it tastes even better than packet soup.  

## Tip

This soup is perfect when you do not have many ingredients at home but still want something warm, quick and homemade.

Enjoy your meal!
