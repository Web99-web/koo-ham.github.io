## Ingredients

- 2 eggs  
- 200 ml milk  
- 150 g flour  
- 1 tablespoon sugar  
- a bit of oil  

## Preparation

1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.  
2. Add the milk and mix well.  
3. Gradually add the flour.  
4. Heat a pan and add a little oil.  
5. Cook thin pancakes until golden brown.  

## Tip

For softer pancakes, add a bit of sparkling water.
