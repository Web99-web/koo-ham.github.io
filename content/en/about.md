# About

Koo-ham.com is a collection of recipes from a busy family that loves good, healthy and varied food, but does not always have much time for long food preparation.

The name Koo-ham is a playful version of the Croatian word **kuham**, which means **I am cooking**. That is exactly what this website is about: cooking real food for real life, without too much complication.

Our idea is simple: to collect recipes that can actually be used in everyday life. These are meals that can be prepared with normal ingredients and in the amount of time we realistically have between work, school, errands and everyday responsibilities.

## How we choose recipes

On Koo-ham, we focus on recipes that are tasty, practical and simple enough to make again. Some are homemade and traditional, some are quick and modern, and some are inspired by flavors from other cuisines.

The goal is not to create a perfect chef-style website, but a useful place for people who want to eat well without spending two hours in the kitchen every day.

## What you will find on Koo-ham

We publish sweet and savory recipes, traditional dishes, quick meals, simple cakes and small kitchen tips.

Koo-ham is for everyone who enjoys good food, but wants recipes that are clear, practical and possible in real life.
