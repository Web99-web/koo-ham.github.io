# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026

Koo-ham.com is a small family project with recipes, kitchen tips and content related to everyday cooking.

## What data we collect

We do not ask visitors to create an account and we do not sell personal data.

If you contact us by email, we process the information you provide, such as your email address and message content, only so we can reply to you.

## Cookies and local storage

The website may use basic technical cookies or local storage required for the website to function properly, improve security, load pages faster and display content correctly.

Koo-ham.com may also use analytics cookies related to Google Analytics, if the visitor gives consent through the cookie banner.

If we add additional tools in the future, such as a newsletter, advertising or affiliate systems, this page will be updated.

## Analytics

Koo-ham.com uses Google Analytics to understand basic website traffic, such as which pages are visited most, which countries visitors come from and how the content is used.

Google Analytics processes data in an aggregated form and helps us improve the content and user experience. We do not use analytics to personally identify individual visitors.

Where consent is required for analytics cookies, they are used only after the visitor gives consent through the cookie banner.

## Newsletter

If we offer a newsletter in the future, email addresses will be stored with the selected email service provider. Users will be able to unsubscribe at any time using the link in each email.

## Advertising and affiliate links

In the future, we may display ads, sponsored content or affiliate links. If a visitor clicks an affiliate link and makes a purchase, we may earn a small commission, at no extra cost to the visitor.

Sponsored or commercial content will be clearly marked where necessary.

## Sharing your data

We do not sell personal data. Technical service providers, such as hosting, CDN, email services or analytics tools, may process data only to provide their services.

## Your rights

Under GDPR, you may request access, correction, deletion or restriction of the processing of your personal data, where such data is processed.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, you can contact us by email listed on the Contact page.
